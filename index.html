Marvel series captivates with
epic storytelling, iconic characters.

  • Marvel Comics, founded in 1939, was rebranded in 1961 with the creation of "The Fantastic Four" by Stan Lee and Jack Kirby, along with other key figures like Steve Ditko shaping the universe.

  • Major superheroes include Spider-Man, Iron Man, Captain America, The Hulk, Thor, and The X-Men, each with unique stories and abilities.

  • Influential comic arcs include "Civil War," "Infinity Gauntlet," and "Secret Wars," shaping the Marvel Universe's narrative and character development.

  • Launched in 2008 with "Iron Man," the MCU is divided into phases, culminating in blockbuster films like "The Avengers," "Black Panther," and "Avengers: Endgame," and expanding into Disney+ series like "WandaVision" and "Loki."

  • Marvel has significantly influenced popular culture, creating a vast and interconnected universe of stories that span comics, films, TV shows, and more, resonating with audiences worldwide.
